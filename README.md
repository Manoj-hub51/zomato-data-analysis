## Key Insights

- Majority of restaurants fall within the mid-price category.  
- Most restaurants are rated between 3.0 and 4.0.  
- Higher pricing does not always correlate with better ratings.  
- Online delivery adoption varies by city.  

---

## Business Recommendations

- Expand delivery services in high-demand cities  
- Improve service quality in lower-rated segments  
- Promote table booking in competitive markets
